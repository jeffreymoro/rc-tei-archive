Copy-text

The sole textual authority for the broadside version of The Devil's Walk remains the single extant copy of 1812 in the Public Record Office (H.O. 42/127, f. 426; citation incorrect in 1989). The document is a single sheet wove paper without visible watermark, originally white but aged to yellow-brown, that (when in reading position) measures 18 1/16 inches high x 14 7/8 inches wide (= 45.85 centimeters x 36.8 cm.).
