Mary Wollstonecraft Shelley

This image is from the poster for the bicentennial conference, "Mary Wollstonecraft Shelley in Her Times" (New York, 22 May-24 May 1997), sponsored by the Keats-Shelley Association of America and others. That image was in turn based on the portrait of by Reginald Easton now in the Bodleian Library, Oxford University.
