Contents of the Quarterly Review, 1809, Vol. I, No. 1

ART. I

Affaires d'Espagne, Nos. 1 to 5. Confédération des Royaumes et Provinces d'Espagne contre Buonaparte, Nos. 1 to 6, &c.
ART. II

Reliques of Robert Burns, consisting chiefly of original Letters, Poems, and Critical Observations on Scottish Songs; collected and published, by R. H. Cromek.
ART. III

Anecdotes of Painters, who have resided or been born in England; with Critical Remarks on their Productions, by Edward Edwards, deceased, late Teacher of Perspective, and Associate, in the Royal Academy; intended as a continuation to the Anecdotes of Painting, by the late Horace, Earl of Orford.
ART. IV

Woman; or Ida of Athens. By Miss Owenson, Author of the "Wild Irish Girl," the "Novice of St. Dominick," &c.
ART. V

A Grammar of the Sanskrita Language, by Charles Wilkins, LL. D. F.R.S.

A Grammar of the Sungskrit Language, composed from the Works of the most esteemed Grammarians, &c. by W. Carey, and

Mr. Colebrooke's Grammar of the Sanskrit Language.
ART. VI

A Translation of the Georgics, of P. Virgilius Maro, with the Original Text and Notes, Critical and Illustrative, of Ancient and Modern Husbandry. By Wm. Stawell, A. M. Rector of Kilmalooda, in the diocese of Cork, &c.
ART. VII

Memoirs of the Life and Writings of Sir Philip Sidney. By Thomas Zouch, D. D. F. L. S. Prebendary of Durham.
ART. VIII

The Credibility of the Jewish Exodus, defended against some Remarks of Edward Gibbon, Esq. and the Edinburgh Reviewers. By the Rev. W. Cockburn, A.M. Christian Advocate in the University of Cambridge, &c.
ART. IX

Speeches of the Right Honourable John Philpot Curran, Master of the Rolls in Ireland, on the late very interesting State Trials.
ART. X

Théorie de l'Action Capillare; par M. Laplace; Supplément au dixième livre du Traité de Mécanique Céleste.
ART. XI

An Essay on Medals; or an Introduction to the Knowledge of Ancient and Modern Coins and Medals, especially those of Greece, Rome, and Britain. By John Pinkerton.
ART. XII

Public Characters of 1809-10.
ART. XIII

Chronicle of the Cid Rodrigo Dias de Bevar, the Campeador; from the Spanish. By Robert Southey.
ART. XIV

A Manual of Analytical Mineralogy, intended to facilitate the Practical Analysis of Minerals. By Frederick Accum.
ART. XV

An Essay on the earlier Part of the Life of Swift. By the Rev. John Barrett, D. D.
ART. XVI

Caledonian Sketches, or a Tour through Scotland. 1807. To which is prefixed, an Explanatory Address to the Public, upon a recent Trial. By Sir John Carr.
ART. XVII

Periodical Accounts, relative to the Baptist Missionary Society, &c.
ART. XVIII

Narrative of the Siege of Zaragoza. By Charles Richard Vaughan, M.B.
