February 20-22, 1997

The Department of English at the University of South Florida announces the Eleventh Annual DeBartolo Conference on Eighteenth-Century Studies, to be held in Tampa, February 20-22, 1997. Several distinguished scholars, including Peter J. Stanlis (Distinguished Professor of Humanities, Emeritus, Rockford College), Moira Ferguson (James E. Ryan Chair in English and Women's Literature, University of Nebraska), and Pat Rogers (DeBartolo Professor in Liberal Arts, University of South Florida) will speak.

The keynote speeches include:

  • Moira Ferguson, "In Commemoriam Mary Wollstonecraft: Tyranny Writ Large in The Wrongs of Women," Friday, Feb. 21.
  • Peter J. Stanlis, "Edmund Burke and the American Revolution: From Empire to Federal Union," Saturday, Feb. 22.
