Bibliography

This part of the editorial apparatus for P.B. Shelley's The Devil's Walk is slightly modified from the way it will appear in The Complete Poetry of Percy Bysshe Shelley, edited by Donald H. Reiman and Neil Fraistat and published by The Johns Hopkins University Press. Included are:

  • Editions of Shelley's Poetry Collated
  • Other Works Cited

Editions of Shelley's Poetry Collated:

1871
Rossetti, William Michael, "Shelley in 1812-13: An Unpublished Poem and other Particulars," Fortnightly Review, New Series IX, No. xlix (January 1871), 67-85.
1876
Forman, Harry Buxton, ed. The Poetical Works of Percy Bysshe Shelley. 4 vols. London: Reeves and Turner, 1876-1877; 1880.
1892
Woodberry, George Edward, ed. The Complete Poetical Works of Percy Bysshe Shelley. Centenary Edition. 4 vols. Boston and New York: Houghton, Mifflin, 1892.
1927
Ingpen, Roger and Walter E. Peck, eds. The Complete Works of Percy Bysshe Shelley. 10 vols. Julian Edition. London: Ernest Benn; New York: Charles Scribner's Sons, 1926-30.
1964
Jones, Frederick L., ed. The Letters of Percy Bysshe Shelley. 2 vols. Oxford: Clarendon Press, 1964.
1970
Hutchinson, Thomas, ed., corrected by G. M. Matthews. The Complete Poetical Works of Percy Bysshe Shelley. Oxford Standard Authors. Oxford: Oxford University Press, 1970.
1972
Rogers, Neville, ed. The Complete Poetical Works of Percy Bysshe Shelley, 1802-1817. 2 vols. Oxford: Clarendon Press, 1972-1975.
1989
Matthews, G. M. and Kelvin Everest, eds. The Poems of Shelley. 1 vol. to date. London: Longman, 1989.
