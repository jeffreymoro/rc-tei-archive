1794.3
A Sure Way to Prevent
The Threatened Invasion by the French
Anon
The Cambridge Intelligencer (February 1, 1794)

If we fight and tax on—for a year or two more,
The French I dare say will ne'er touch on our shore,
For fear of the charge of maintaining the Poor!
