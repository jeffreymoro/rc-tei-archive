2984. Robert Southey to John Hookham Frere, [5 May 1817]

Address: To/ J. H. Frere Esqrre/ Fleming Villa/ Brompton
Stamped: [partial] Unpaid/ 88 Oxford
Postmarks: 2 oClock/ 5 MY/1817 AM; 4 oClock/ 5 MY/ 1817 EV
Seal: red wax; arm raising aloft cross of Lorraine
Endorsement: Southey/ 1817
MS: Beinecke Library, Osborn MSS File ‘S’, Folder 14121. ALS; 2p.
Unpublished.
Dating note: Dating from postmark.

Monday morning
My dear Sir

I will wait on you with much pleasure on Wednesday at 7 o clock

Believe me Yrs with sincere respect
Robert Southey
