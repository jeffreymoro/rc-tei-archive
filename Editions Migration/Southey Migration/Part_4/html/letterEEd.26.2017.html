2017. Robert Southey to Grosvenor Charles Bedford, 14 January 1812

Address: To/ G. C. Bedford Esqr/ [in another hand] 9 Stafford Row/ J. C. Herries
Endorsement: 14 Jany 1812
MS: Bodleian Library, MS Eng. Lett. c. 24
Unpublished.

Dear Grosvenor

Your provoking Phillidor

(1)
Unidentified.
after having long annoyed me by being everlastingly in the way, has contrived to get out of the way once more just when I had an opportunity of getting rid of him.

Mrs C., in her unhappy condi state, frequently abuses you, & when I ask her what she has to say to you where I write, desires me to give her hate. Notwithstanding which odious message, the said personage troubles you with a letter pro posto two-pennyano.

(2)
i.e. for the twopenny post.

RS.

Jany 14. 1812.
