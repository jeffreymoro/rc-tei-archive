2676. Robert Southey to Henry Herbert Southey, [6 December 1815]

Address: To/ Dr Southey/ 15. Queen Anne Street/ Cavendish Square/ London
Stamped: KESWICK/ 298
Postmark: E/ 9 DE 9/ 1815
MS: Bodleian Library, Don. d. 3
Unpublished.

My dear Harry

Here we are safe & sound, – Edith the better for her journeys, – all well, – & this is all I have time to say. Here is a house full of confusion & happiness – the difference between unpacking & packing! Our love to Louisa. Would that there were a prospect of her coming here that we might repay the hospitalities we found –

God bless you
RS.

Keswick. Wednesday.
