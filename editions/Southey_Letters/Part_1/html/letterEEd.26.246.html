My dear Grosvenor

I have but time to say that is still possible, tho not likely, that my Mother may live with me. that we may perhaps find a difficulty in furnishing our own apartment on our return. here you see are two obstacles which cannot be immediately removed. I should much wish to domesticate with you if they were out of the way.

God bless you.
Robert Southey.
