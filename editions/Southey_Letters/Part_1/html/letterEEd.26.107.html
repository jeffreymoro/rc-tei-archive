107. Robert Southey to Grosvenor Charles Bedford, 13 October 1794

(1)
MS: Brown University LibraryUnpublished
Grosvenor Charles Bedford,

I particularly want the Minstrel — Sayers

(1)
James Beattie (1735–1803; DNB), The Minstrel (1771–1774) and Frank Sayers (1763–1817; DNB), Poems (1792).
— & the two volumes of the British Album.
(2)
The British Album (1792–1793), consisting of reprints of the ‘Della Cruscan’ poets, especially Robert Merry (1752–1798; DNB).
I have so often said this in letters of my wonted length & you so often overlooked it that to write merely to request them is the only means of succeeding. the

send these books to No 8 Westgate Buildings Bath. I want not the great coat nor the boots.

when they arrive you may expect a long letter from me.

yours as usual

only a little irate

Robert Southey

Monday 13 Oct. Bath.

1794.
