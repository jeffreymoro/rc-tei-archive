276. Robert Southey to Grosvenor Charles Bedford, 4 December 1797

(1)
Address: To/ G C Bedford Esqr/ Exchequer.Postmark: ‘12 oClock/ 5. DE/ 97 NOON’ Watermark: TMS: Bodleian Library, MS Eng. Lett. c. 23Unpublished.
Grosvenor Bedford

As our intimacy seems to be in proportion to our distance from each other, I pray you think that I am a thousand miles off.

R. S.

Monday evening.

Dec. 4. 97.
