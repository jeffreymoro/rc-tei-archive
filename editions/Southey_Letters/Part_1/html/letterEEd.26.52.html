52. Robert Southey to Grosvenor Charles Bedford, [c. 21 June 1793]

(1)
Address: C G Bedford Esqr/ Old Palace Yard/ WestminsterStamped: OXFORDPostmark: AJU/ 22/ 93Watermark: Figure of BritanniaEndorsement: 21 June 1793 MS: Bodleian Library, MS Eng. Lett. c. 22Unpublished.

£

C Collins has just got an exhibition worth 25 per an, for 15 years.

that line is worth 4d.

post just going vidi. Friday night — June 21 1793.

(1)
June 21 1793: Written in another hand.

next Tuesday we meet at tea at my rooms.


Smith

Thos. Stevenson

Geo. Bernard

Chas Simpson

Ben. Couch

Jas. Mineur

W. Bibbins

Hy. Brougham

Ed. Colman

Caven de Lane


Walpole in Clico Consel defferd fs ad dd &. ?an.

Geo. Chetwynd. — 62.

Stephen Cottrell

Willm. Fairpence

(2)
Probably an in-joke, as none of these individuals were friends of Southey’s at either Westminster School or Oxford.
