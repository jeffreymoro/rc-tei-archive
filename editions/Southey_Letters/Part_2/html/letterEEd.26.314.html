Bath. May. 11. 98.
My dear Strachey

I learn from Wynn that you are shortly about to quit the kingdom, so shortly indeed that I shall not see you before your departure. but you must not go without receiving my wishes for your welfare. little intimate as we have been of late years, I have always thought of you with affection â€“ & feel now that I am losing a friend.

I have desired Cottle to forward my book

God bless you Strachey. & may you return from India with as uncorrupted a heart as you will carry there!

yrs truly & affectionately
Robert Southey.
