660. Robert Southey to Richard Sharp, 24 February 1802

Address: R. Sharp Esqr/ 17. Mark Lane
Stamped: [illegible]
Watermark: [obscured by visiting card with Southey’s signature which has been pasted onto the address leaf, possibly] T. BUDGEN
Endorsement: Mr. Southey the poet
MS: Newnham College, Cambridge, Harold Young Papers 382b
Unpublished.

Dr Sir

I beg pardon for not replying to your first note. it was my intention to have answerd it in person. I shall do th myself the pleasure of accompanying Coleridge –

I remain Sir yrs respectfully
Robert Southey.

Wednesday.

24 Feby 1802
