436. Robert Southey to Thomas Poole, 21 September [1799]

Address: To/ Mr T Poole/ Stowey/ near Bridgewater/ Single
Stamped: [twice, one a partial stamping] Exeter
Endorsement: fr Southey/ Septr 21st 99
MS: British Library, Add MS 35344
Unpublished.

My dear Sir

Yours inclosing a five guinea bill, reachd me this morning. the parcel also for Coleridge arrived & shall be forwarded to him. he has returned to Ottery, but I have the expectation of seeing him here again. Edith joins me in remembrances. <yours with respect.>

xxxxxxxx xxxxxxxx                         Robert Southey

Exeter. Saturday 21. Sept.
