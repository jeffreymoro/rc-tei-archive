623. Robert Southey to Barbara Seton, [late October/ early November 1801]

MS: MS untraced; the letter is mentioned – but not quoted from – in Ifan Kyrle Fletcher, ‘Robert Southey and Miss Seton’, Times Literary Supplement, no. 1868 (20 November 1937), 896. Southey’s letters to Seton were advertised for sale in Kyrle Fletcher’s catalogue no. 57 (1936), Item 332. Their purchaser and current location is unknown.
