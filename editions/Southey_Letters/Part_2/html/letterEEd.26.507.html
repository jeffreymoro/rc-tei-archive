507. Robert Southey to Joseph Cottle, [c. 6 April 1800]

(1)
Address: To/ Mr Cottle/ Gloucester Street/ Brunswick Square/ Bristol./ Single SheetEndorsements: April 6 1800; 2 (115); 1800 1799 MS: Bristol Reference Library, B20857Unpublished.

Lend me Coleridges second edition.

(1)
Coleridge’s Poems (1797).

Which is just now in requisition

For I want a passage by <to> quote,

And have not got it by rote.

And the volume shall be returned duly

By Robert Southey, yours truly.
