2752. Robert Southey to Henry Herbert Southey, [9 April 1816]

(1)
Address: To/ Dr Southey/ Queen Ann Street/ Cavendish Square/ London Stamped: KESWICK/ 298 Postmark: E/ 12 AP 12/ 1816 Seal: red wax MS: Bodleian Library, MS Don. d. 3. ALS; 2p. Unpublished.
My dear Harry

The last 24 hours have proved on the whole favourable.

(1)
Southey’s only son Herbert was gravely ill and died on 17 April 1816.
Edmondson & Miss Barker (whose opinion is worth quite as much) both believe that the disease is subdued, & only debility remaining. For myself I still perceive the cough which was the first symptom, & observe that the pain at the breast, which has been the most alarming, exists still, tho materially relieved. Would that you had been with us. Out of danger it is impossible to pronounce him – but there is hope

God bless you
RS.

Tuesday.
