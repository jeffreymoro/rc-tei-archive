3007. Robert Southey to Grosvenor Charles Bedford, [7 August 1817]

(1)
Address: To/ G. C. Bedford Esqre/ Exchequer/ Westminster Stamped: [partial] DOVER Postmark: A/ 8 AU 8/ 1817 Endorsements: 7 August 1817; 7 August. 1817. MS: Bodleian Library, MS Eng. Lett. d. 47. ALS; 2p. Unpublished. Dating note: Dating from endorsement.
Thursday. Dover.
My dear Grosvenor

Safe & sound. Going to look for a letter from you, – then to the Custom House – & then to set off. We mean to sleep at Rochester at night – & hope to reach Q Anne Street tomorrow by noon

God bless you
RS.
