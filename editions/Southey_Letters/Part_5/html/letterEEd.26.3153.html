3153. Robert Southey to William Hawkes Smith,16 June 1818

Endorsement: To Mr Hawks Smith/ Birmingham
MS: McLennan Library, McGill University. ALS; 1p. (c).
Unpublished.

Keswick. 16 June 1818
Dear Sir

Yesterday I received your parcel. Accept my thanks for the bound copy. The first plate, as it now stands, is a very pleasing one, & I hope the book will obtain the reputation & the success which it deserves.

(1)
William Hawkes Smith’s Essays in Design … Illustrative of the Poem of ‘Thalaba the Destroyer’ (1818), no. 2612 in the sale catalogue of Southey’s library. The first (of twenty-seven) plates in the volume was entitled ‘The Desert’.

When next I write to Messrs Longman I will desire them to pay over the price of six copies from my account to yours.

Believe me Sir with sincere respect your obliged & obedient
Robert Southey.
