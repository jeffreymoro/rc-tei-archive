3157. Robert Southey to [Edward] Everett, [30 June 1818]

(1)
Address: To/ – Everett Esqre/ Queens Head Endorsement: 1818/ June 30 MS: Massachusetts Historical Society. AL; 1p. (c). Unpublished. Dating note: Dating from endorsement.

Mr Southey requests Mr Everett will do him the favour of drinking tea with him this evening, as soon after six o clock as may suit his convenience.

Tuesday. 4 o clock.
