2759. Robert Southey to Thomas Southey, [17 April 1816]

Address: To/ Capt Southey RN/ Warcop Hall/ near/ Appleby
Stamped: KESWICK/ 298
MS: British Library, Add MS 47890. ALS; 2p.
Unpublished.
Dating note: Dating from content, the letter was written immediately after the death of Herbert Southey on Wednesday 17 April 1817.

Wednesday
My dear Tom

My last will have taught you what to expect. All is over. God has been merciful to us – made his passage easy. I will not tell you what I have lost, – but even at the worst I am truly thankful for having had such a child, – & as long as I live one of my deepest pleasures will be the prospect of meeting him again.

(1)
Southey’s son Herbert had just died.

God grant that you may be spared such affliction as this.
RS.
