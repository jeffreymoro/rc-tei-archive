2996. Robert Southey to Henry Herbert Southey, 11 May 1817

(1)
Address: To/ Dr Southey/ 15 Queen Anne Street/ Cavendish Square/ London Stamped: F 61 P Postmark: FPO/ MY 13/ 1817 MS: Bodleian Library, MS Don. d. 4. ALS; 2p. Unpublished.
Calais.Sunday morning.11 May 1817
My dear Harry

Here we are after a slow but easy passage of 14 hours, – having lain all night off the mouth of the harbour. Give Bedford a line to notify my safe arrival, it will save him postage & save me time.

Let me find a letter at the Post Office Geneva, – before we arrive there I think you will have news to tell me, which I shall be anxious to learn.

(1)
The birth of Henry Herbert Southey’s first son, Robert Southey, on 1 June 1817.
Direct there if it happens before this day month, – if later to Brussels.

Love to Louisa & Mrs Gonne –

God bless you
RS
