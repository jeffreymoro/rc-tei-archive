2073. Robert Southey to John Morgan [fragment], [c. 6 April 1812]

(1)
Address: To/ J J Morgan Esqr/ 71. Berners Street/ Oxford Street/ LondonStamped: KESWICK/ 298Postmark: E/ 6 AP 6/ 1812Endorsement: R. Southey EsqrMS: University of Kentucky LibraryUnpublished.Dating note: Dating from postmark.

[MS missing] I shall not be in town till the close of the year, & then I shall often find my way to Berners Street. Remember us to Mrs Morgan & her sister,

(1)
Charlotte Brent (dates unknown).
& if Coleridge be arrived tell him to write soon [MS torn] drop a tear for Betty Amlett.
(2)
John Mayne (1759–1836), ‘Betty Amlett. An Elegiac Ballad’, line 1, published in Monthly Magazine, 33 (March 1812), 149. It told the story of a woman sentenced to death for drowning her illegitimate baby.

Yrs very truly
R Southey.
