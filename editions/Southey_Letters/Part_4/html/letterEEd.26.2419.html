2419. Robert Southey to John Rickman, 9 May 1814

(1)
Address: To/ J Rickman EsqreEndorsement: RS/ 9 May 1814MS: Huntington Library, RS 225Unpublished.
My dear R.

I trouble you with two inclosures, – one for the Twopenny, – the other (which is doubled xxxx into letter-form) for the Exchequer.

The death of Danvers will cast a gloom over the recollections of half my life.

(1)
Danvers died on 3 May 1814.
– The loss of such a man is like an amputation – the wound heals, – but the limb will always be wanting.

Remember me to Mrs R. &God bless you
RS.

May 9. 1814
