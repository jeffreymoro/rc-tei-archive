2184. Robert Southey to John Murray, 25 November [1812]

Address: To/ Mr Murray
Endorsement: 1812 Novr 25th/ Southey R –
MS: National Library of Scotland, MS 42550
Unpublished.

My dear Sir

I find the proofs of Nelson

(1)
Life of Nelson (1813).
have not been sent, as I wished them to be, to my brother. I am very desirous that they should pass under a sailors eye, – blunders may easily escape me, in spite of all my care, which he would immediately detect. – Mr Croker will I dare say have the goodness to frank to him the sheets which are struck off: we may make an errata of any triffling errors, & I believe nothing will be found so lubberly as to require a cancel.

Capt Southey, St Helens, – Auckland is my brothers direction.

Yrs very truly
R Southey.

Nov 25.
