2486. Robert Southey to Grosvenor Charles Bedford, 14 October [1814]

Endorsements: 14 Oct 1814; With 1st Chapter of 2 Vol: of History of Brazil; 14 . Octr 1814
MS: Bodleian Library, MS Eng. Lett. c. 25
Unpublished.

My dear Grosvenor

Will you transmit the mss.

(1)
The MS was the first chapter of the second volume of the History of Brazil, published in 1817.
to Streatham per Docstorum – or mount Orelio
(2)
A war horse in Roderick, the Last of the Goths (1814), Book 25.
& carry it there yourself.

I have been ten days from home & am returned now, on my return, fairly sat down to the winters campaign. – When shall I see Barre’s remains?

(3)
Grosvenor Bedford’s Letters and Miscellaneous Papers … With a Memoir of His Life (1814) of his cousin Barré Charles Roberts, who had died in 1810 aged 21. It was reviewed by Southey in the Quarterly Review, 12 (January 1815), 509–519.
– You are more sanguine respecting Roderick than I am.

in haste
RS.

Keswick. 14 Oct.
