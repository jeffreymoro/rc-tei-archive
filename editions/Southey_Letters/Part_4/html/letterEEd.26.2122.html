Keswick.July 8. 1812.
My dear Sir

While I am availing myself of your official character for the purpose of covering a communication to our friend Mr Gifford, I must at the same time return my thanks to you individually for your late kindness, which led you to take more trouble than I had anticipated. I have laid your letter
