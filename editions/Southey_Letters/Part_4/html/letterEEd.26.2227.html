2227. Robert Southey to Grosvenor Charles Bedford, 5 March 1813

(1)
Address: To/ G. C. Bedford Esqre/ ExchequerEndorsement: 5. March 1813MS: Bodleian Library, MS Eng. Lett. c. 25Unpublished.
Dear Grosvenor

I have just now no more time than to make up a packet for the Grand Parleur,

(1)
The Speaker, Charles Abbot; i.e. this letter was sent via a frank from Abbot.
– to beg that you will put this note for the Long Men of the Row into the Twopenny post, – & to tell you that I have, to my great comfort, mounted a pair of Cumberland clogs, in which I clatter away thro the streets of Keswick, & defy all dirt.

RS.

March 5. 1813.
