2566. Robert Southey to William Gifford, 5 March 1815

(1)
Address: Wm Gifford EsqreSeal: Traces of red wax sealWatermark: Ivy Mill 1810MS: The private collection of Frederick BurwickUnpublished.
5. March 1815
My dear Sir

I send

(1)
This letter was inclosed in Southey to Grosvenor Charles Bedford, [4–]5 March 1815, Letter 2565.
you a few pages respecting poor Barré Roberts, whose papers have <made> me respect him almost as much as if he had been personally known to me. The extracts will make the article amusing, – the mere fact that the book is unpublished gives a value to it & he has some claim to this little mark of respect in your journal.
(2)
Southey’s review of Letters and Miscellaneous Papers of Barré Charles Roberts, Student of Christ Church, Oxford, with a Memoir of his Life appeared in Quarterly Review, 12 (January 1815), 509–519.

I have much in hand for you & xx more in mind.

Believe me my dear SirVery truly & respectfully yours
Robert Southey
