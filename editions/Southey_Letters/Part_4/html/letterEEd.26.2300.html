2300. Robert Southey to Grosvenor Charles Bedford, [10] September 1813

Address: To/ G. C. Bedford Esqr/ 9 Stafford Row/ Buckingham Gate
Postmark: 12 o’Clock/ SP 11/ 1813 Nn
Endorsement: Septr 1813; Sept 1811. 1813
MS: Bodleian Library, MS Eng. Lett. c. 25, fol. 34-35
Unpublished.
Dating note: Friday was 10 September in 1813.

My dear Grosvenor

It may be convenient to you to know that Harry the Doctor cannot dine with you tomorrow; having Your note had not arrived when he left here this morning, & he has engaged himself – but he will endeavour to come in the evening. – I am not quite easy about the state in which you were yesterday when we parted.

Yrs
RS.

4 o clock Friday Streatham
