2171. Robert Southey to Grosvenor Charles Bedford, 3 November 1812

Endorsement: 3 Nov. 1812
MS: Bodleian Library, MS Eng. Lett. c. 24
Unpublished.

Dear Grosvenor

I had a daughter born yesterday, & all’s well.

You may keep these mss.

(1)
A MS draft of part of Roderick, the Last of the Goths (1814).
as long as you please. Show them to Gifford & report to me his remarks. And when you have entirely done with them, consign them over to the Doctor.

Sendez-vous me some money as soon as you can, for I am pauperrimus

(2)
Southey was ‘very poor’ because of his difficulties in getting payment from John Ballantyne for his work on the Edinburgh Annual Register.

RS.

Nov 3. 1812.
