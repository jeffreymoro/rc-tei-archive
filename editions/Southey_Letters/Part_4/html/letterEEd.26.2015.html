2015. Robert Southey to Grosvenor Charles Bedford, 11 January 1812

Address: To/ G. C. Bedford Esqr./ [in another hand] Exchequer/ J. C. Herries
Endorsement: 11 Jany 1812
MS: Bodleian Library, MS Eng. Lett. c. 24
Unpublished.

My dear Grosvenor

I left your last letter unanswered, – not from indifference to it, but because another subject ran away with all the time & paper I had to bestow. And now I have neither the one nor the other. Only – be assured the true physician for all your mental maladies is – the Butler.

(1)
A private joke between Southey and Bedford concerning a comic hero they had invented called ‘the Butler’.
Write that book about the Butler which I have been for so many years calling upon you to write.
(2)
This was not written.

RS.

Jany 11. 1812.
