2380. Robert Southey to William Gifford, 18 February 1814

Address: To/ Wm Gifford Esqre
MS: Beinecke Library, Osborn MSS File ‘S’, Folder 14124
Unpublished.

Keswick. Feby. 18. 1814.
My dear Sir

I send you an account of the Nicobars

(1)
Johann Gottfried Haensel (1749–1814), Letters on the Nicobar Islands (1812), reviewed by Southey in Quarterly Review, 11 (April 1814), 57–72.
to which I have brought all the knowledge I could find.

I take the liberty of inclosing a packet for Grosvenor Bedford, that the same potential direction may carry it free.

(2)
The inclosure was Southey to Charles Grosvenor Bedford, 18 February 1814, Letter 2379.
– The snow has left us, & winter tho it has seldom been so rigorous, was never more beautiful than it is among these mountains at this time.

Believe me my dear Sir Yrs very truly
Robert Southey.
