2536. Robert Southey to Charles Watkin Williams Wynn, [late 1814]

(1)
MS: National Library of Wales, MS 4813DUnpublished.Dating note: This letter is difficult to date, but from its content probably belongs to late 1814.
My dear C

Let Harry (my brother) – have the Rodericks

(1)
Copies of Roderick, the Last of the Goths (1814).
as soon as you can. – I have a letter of explanatory & apologetical shuffle from Ballantyne. so far satisfactory as it tells me the Bill is by this time paid.

RS.
