1935. Robert Southey to Grosvenor Charles Bedford, 11 June 1811

(1)
Address: To/ G. C. Bedford EsqrEndorsement: 11 June 1811MS: Bodleian Library, MS Eng. Lett. c. 24Unpublished.
My dear G.

There has not been during the last three weeks one single day dry enough for hunting foxes-tails on the fells.

In two hours we start. Miserere mei!

(1)
‘Have pity on me’.
Οιμοι, οιμοι, οτοττοτοι!
(2)
‘O me, o me, lackaday’.
– I am very uncomfortable at the prospect

RS.

Tuesday June 11. 1811
