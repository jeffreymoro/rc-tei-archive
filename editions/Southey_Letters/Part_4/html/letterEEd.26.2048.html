2048. Robert Southey to John Murray, 25 February [1812]

Address: To/ Mr Murray/ Fleet Street/ London./ Single Proof Sheet inclosed.Stamped: KESWICK/ 298Postmark: E/ 28 FE 28/ 1812Watermark: 18[MS cut]6Endorsement: 1811 Feb 25/ Southey R – MS: National Library of Scotland, MS 42550Unpublished.Dating note: misdated 1811 by Southey; the content makes it clear this letter dates from 1812.
Keswick.Feby. 25. 1811
(1)
Southey misdates, the letter belongs to 1812.
My dear Sir

The paragraph which I missed too late in the sheet M. has made its appearance here p. 147.

(2)
A proof of The Origin, Nature, and Object, of the New System of Education (1812).
I shall be glad if it can be restored to its proper place, – but if that, as I fear, is the case, I have adapted it as well as the thing allowed to its present situation. – Tomorrow the two concluding proofs will be returned together with the Postscript, – & a dedication if I can compass one, which is very doubtful. – If I do it will be in blank verse. – I have been all day employed upon these sheets.

Mr Rodds book

(3)
Thomas Rodd (1763–1822; DNB), bookseller, poet and translator. His History of Charles the Great and Orlando, and Other Spanish Ballads was published in 1812.
shall be returned as soon & as carefully as possible.

Yrs very truly
R Southey.
