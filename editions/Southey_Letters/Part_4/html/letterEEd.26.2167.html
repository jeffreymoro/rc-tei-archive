with some books for Miss Jephson.

(1)
Mrs Smith’s granddaughter Charlotte-Julia Jephson (dates unknown). Her father was Richard Mountney Jephson (1768–1824), Irish politician and lawyer, and nephew of the playwright Robert Jephson (1736/7–1803; DNB). He was an Admiralty Judge, Judge-Advocate at Gibraltar and 1st baronet.
