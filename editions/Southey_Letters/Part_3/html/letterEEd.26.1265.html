1265. Robert Southey to Henry Herbert Southey, 24 January [1807]

(1)
Address: To/ H. H. Southey MD./ 11. Craven Street/ StrandPostmark: 4 o’Clock/ 27 JA/ 1807 EL Seal: [partial]MS: Bodleian Library, MS Don. D3Unpublished.
Sir Domine Doctor Ignorantissime

Herein are three drafts for so many acquaintances worth making on all accounts. Tuffins house is the corner either of Upper Grosvenor or Upper Brook Street – I forget which – the side nearest Piccadilly, – & the door in Park Lane. – I shall send you a letter to Mrs Gonne as soon as Edith has written it, & you would have had these sooner but I have been unwell these last three days with a sort of Influenza which however is leaving me – I forgot to send your 7–6 in the parcel – but will frank it when I get a score shilling piece.

God bless you
RS.

Saturday 24 Jany
