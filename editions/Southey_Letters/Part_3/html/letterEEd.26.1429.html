1429. Robert Southey to John Rickman, 25 [February 1808]

Address: To/ John Rickman Esqr
Endorsement: Feb –/ 1808
MS: Huntington Library, RS 127
Unpublished.
Dating note: dating from JR’s endorsement; RS gives ‘Thursday 25’

My dear Rickman

If there be room in the mail which passes thro Lichfield on Sunday evening I shall get in & you will see me (God willing) on the following morning. I have been snow-drifted to Liverpool, & detained there a few days for the sake of seeing Roscoe & the other remarkables of the place.

RS.

Teddesley Park. Staffordshire

Thursday 25.
