1106. Robert Southey to Humphrey Senhouse, [September 1805]

(1)
Address: – Senhouse Esqr.Seal: [illegible] red waxEndorsements: Septr 1805/ Robt Southey to HS; Robt Southey to HSMS: University of Rochester, A.S727 1:1 Unpublished.
Dear Sir

Will you & the Ladies do us the favour of dining with us on Friday next, at 4 o-clock?

yrs very respectfully
Robert Southey.

Wednesday evening.
