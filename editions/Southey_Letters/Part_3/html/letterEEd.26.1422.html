1422. Robert Southey to Grosvenor Charles Bedford, 9 February 1808

(1)
Address: To/ G. C. Bedford Esqr/ Exchequer/ WestminsterEndorsement: 9 Feby 1808Stamped: KESWICK/ 298Postmarks: E/ FEB/ 10MS: Bodleian Library, Eng. Lett. c. 24Unpublished.
My dear Grosvenor

I have a daughter Emma born into the world this afternoon, & a bouncing daughter she is.

You will see me in about 18 days, for I shall be a fortnight on the road. If meantime there be any of thing forthcoming from the Exchequer to the most loyal & deserving of his Majestys pensioners, do you forward it as tho I were at home, – it will come seasonably to an empty purse –

God bless you
RS.

Feby 9. 1808.
