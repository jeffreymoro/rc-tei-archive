4114. Robert Southey to Aaron Hartwell, 1 January 1824

(1)
Address: To/ Aaron Hartwell Esqre/ Bristol Institution/ Bristol Postmark: G J A/ 2/ 1824 Endorsements: R. Southey Esq; No. 2 in the list, &c MS: Bristol Record Office, 32079/148 Unpublished.
London. 1 Jany. 1824
Sir

I have just received your letter of Dec. 19 announcing that the Bristol Philosophical & Literary Society

(1)
Members of the Bristol Literary and Philosophical Institution (founded 1820) had just established a Philosophical and Literary Society, where local members might read papers to each other.
have been pleased to elect me as one of their Honorary Members. Honours of this kind must always be agreable to those who endeavour to deserve them: & this is peculiarly so to me as coming from my native city.

I have the honour to remain Sir your obedient servant
Robert Southey.
