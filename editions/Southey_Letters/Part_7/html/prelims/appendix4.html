Appendix 4

Southey’s manuscript drafts of his letter to the Courier 1824

This Appendix makes available annotated transcripts of two manuscripts, Huntington Library, HM 6656, relating to Southey’s letter to the Editor of the Courier (see Letter 4289), published in the Courier on 13 December 1824. Southey’s addition of his own footnotes means that both drafts, especially 4.1, have complex layouts. We have therefore used folio numbers, in square brackets, to show this and Southey’s drafting process.
