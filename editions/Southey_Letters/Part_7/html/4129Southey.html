4129. Robert Southey to Grosvenor Charles Bedford, 8 February [1824]

(1)
Address: To/ G. C. Bedford Esqre./ 9 Stafford Row <Exchequer>/ Buckingham Gate <Westminster> Postmarks: [partial] HMOND/ Mg/ 9 FE/ 1824; 12. NOON. 12/ FE. 9/ 1824 Endorsements: 8 Feb 1824; 8 Feby 1824 MS: Bodleian Library, MS Eng. Lett. c. 26. ALS; 2p. Unpublished.
My dear G.

Any farther arrangement with Chantrey

(1)
Sir Francis Chantrey wished to sculpt Southey. He did so in 1828.
is impracticable on my part, even if it were otherwise on his. I am sorry for it, for I should like much to have past a few hours in his company. My place is taken for Friday, – will you give me a beef stake on Tuesday, & ask nobody to meet me, that I may have a quiet comfortable evening with you & the Magister Rotulorum & Miss Page? – Answer by a line to Q. Anne Street, whither I return that morning.

God bless you
RS.

Richmond. Sunday 8 Feby
