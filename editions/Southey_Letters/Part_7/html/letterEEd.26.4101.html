4101. Robert Southey to John Murray, [9 December 1823]

Address: To/ John Murray Esqre/ Albemarle Street
MS:Gen MSS [Misc], Princeton University Library. ALS; 2p.
Unpublished.
Dating note: Dating from content. The letter was written on the Tuesday before Friday 12 December when Southey went to Montreal House, Sevenoaks, Kent.

My dear Sir

On Friday I go into Kent with Herries. Monday I return for the Westminster Play,

(1)
Every December the King’s Scholars at Westminster School, London, gave three performances of a play in Latin. In 1823 it was The Adelphi by Publius Terentius Afer (c. 195/185–159 BC).
& will stay in town on the Tuesday to have the pleasure of dining with you, – if that day should suit you.

yrs very truly
Robert Southey

Q Anne Street. Tuesday afternoon

Notes
1.. Every December the King’s Scholars at Westminster School, London, gave three performances of a play in Latin. In 1823 it was The Adelphi by Publius Terentius Afer (c. 195/185–159 BC).[back]
