4100. Robert Southey to John Abraham Heraud, [c. 5 December 1822]

(1)
Address: J.A. Heraud Esqre/ 13. Guildford Street/ Wilmington Square/ Spa Fields/ Paid Stamped: Berners St/ 3py P.Paid Postmark: 3 P PAID/ 5 DE/ 7 NIGHT 7 Endorsement: 6th Decr. 1822 MS: Berg Collection, New York Public Library. ALS; 2p. Unpublished. Dating note: Dating from content. Southey was in London in 1823 and not 1822, as suggested by the endorsement.
My dear Sir

During my hurried visits to London, every day & almost every hour has its engagement. I will breakfast with you on Friday (this day week) – & be with you as near half past eight, as a doubtful knowledge of the distance & of the way will permit, – guessing it at half an hours walk for a long legged man, who never loses time about any thing.

Yrs very truly
R Southey

Friday morning.
