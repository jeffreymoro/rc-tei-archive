3341. Robert Southey to William Lowther, 1st Earl of Lonsdale, 2 August 1819

(1)
MS: Cumbria Record Office, Carlisle, DLons/L1/2/60a. ALS; 1p. Unpublished.
August 2. 1819.
My Lord

A long standing engagement takes me into Scotland next week, to visit the Caledonian Canal, with Mr Telford & Mr Rickman.

(1)
Southey’s tour of Scotland lasted from 17 August until 1 October 1819. For his record of events, see Journal of a Tour in Scotland in 1819, ed. Charles Harold Herford (1929).
Both my companions being men of business, my absence will not be long, – & it will give me great pleasure to obey your Lordships summons should I return in time.

I am My Lordwith great respectyr Lordships obliged & obedient Servt
Robert Southey.
