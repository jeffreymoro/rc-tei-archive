3655. Robert Southey to H. S. Jensort, 17 March 1821

(1)
Address: To/ H. S. Jensort/ Post-Office/ Liverpool Stamped: KESWICK/ 298 MS: Tipped into a copy of Southey’s The Life of Wesley; and the Rise and Progress of Methodism, 2 vols (1820), vol. I, before the title page, in The Methodist Collection, Drew University Library. ALS; 2p. (c). Unpublished.
Keswick.17 March. 1821
Sir

I am very much obliged to you for your offer of Mr Wesleys papers.

(1)
Presumably papers relating to John Wesley (1703–1791; DNB).
I shall be exceedingly glad to inspect them. They may very probably enable me to correct & amend a work
(2)
Southey’s The Life of Wesley; and the Rise and Progress of Methodism (1820).
on which great pains have been bestowed.

I am Siryr obliged & obedient servant
Robert Southey.
