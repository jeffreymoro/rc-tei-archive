3277. Robert Southey to [Unknown Correspondent] [fragment], 29 March [1819]

MS: Department of Rare Books, Special Collections and Preservation, River Campus Libraries, University of Rochester, Robert Southey Papers A.S727. ALS; 1p.R
Unpublished.

I am right glad to hear that Peter Bell is to make his appearance before the public.

(1)
Wordsworth’s Peter Bell, A Tale in Verse (1819), published by Longman and dedicated to Southey.
& I should be more glad if Wordsworth were going to London before the occasion, that we might travel together. I expect to start the first week in May.

For xxxx xx xxxxxxxxxx xxxxxx xxxx xxxxxxx xxxxxxxx you

God bless you
RS.

Monday 29 March.
