3252. Robert Southey to Henry Herbert Southey, 24 February 1819

(1)
Address: To/ Dr Southey/ Queen Anne Street/ Cavendish Square/ London Stamped: KESWICK/ 298 Postmark: E/ 27 FE 27/ 1819 Seal: [partial] red wax; arm raising aloft cross of Lorraine MS: Keswick Museum and Art Gallery, KESMG 1996.5.97. ALS; 2p. Unpublished.
My dear Harry

Edith is in bed with a son after a tremendous labour. The child is one of the largest that Edmondson ever saw born into the world, & came stern foremost. At one he believed it would be lost, & was under no little apprehension for the mother. Thank God all seems to be going on well at last.

Our love to Louisa & Mrs Gonne.

God bless you
RS.

Wednesday. 24 Feby 1819.
