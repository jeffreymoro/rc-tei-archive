3298. Robert Southey to [Hans] Busk, 21 May 1819

(1)
Address: To/ H Busk Esqr/ 31. Nottingham Place/ London Stamped: KESWICK/ 298 Postmark: [partial] F/ 24 MY 24 MS: Berg Collection, New York Public Library. ALS; 2p. Unpublished.
Keswick.21 May. 1819.
Sir

I thank you for the pleasure which I have received from the pleasant sequel of your Banquet.

(1)
Busk’s The Dessert; a Poem, to which is added The Tea (1819), the sequel to The Banquet, in Three Cantos (1819).
It reached me this morning, & I admire in it the same playful elegance & talent which ensured for its predecessor so favourable a reception from the public.

Believe me SirYr obliged & faithful servant
Robert Southey
