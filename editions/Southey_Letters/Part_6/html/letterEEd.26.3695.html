3695. Robert Southey to [John] Morrison, 19 June 1821

Address: To/ Mr Morrison/ Maryport
Endorsement: 19th June 1821 –/ Mr Southey Greta/ Balce of Rent –
Stamped: KESWICK/298
MS: Pforzheimer Collection, New York Public Library, Misc MS. 3578. ALS; 2p.
Unpublished.

Keswick. 19 June. 1821
Sir

Retaining Thirty Pounds from my Whitsuntide Rent

(1)
The rent for Greta Hall, the Southeys’ home.
(payable to Mr Hartley Coleridge in part of his claims, if the Annuitants are otherwise satisfied)
(2)
William Jackson had left Hartley Coleridge an annuity in his will, charged on the Greta Hall estate.
& deducting the sums of 6/5d paid for repairing the roof, & 14/6 for making, painting & putting up new spouts, – I remit you a draft for the residuary <remaining> balance of £6–9-1

& am Sir Yrs &c
Robert Southey.
Notes
1.. The rent for Greta Hall, the Southeys’ home.[back]
2.. William Jackson had left Hartley Coleridge an annuity in his will, charged on the Greta Hall estate.[back]
